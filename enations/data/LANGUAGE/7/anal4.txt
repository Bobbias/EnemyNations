This is the shareware version of Enemy Nations and it has a number of limitations in it:
1)   It only includes 8-bit (256 color) art.
2)   It only includes MIDI music. No digital music.
3)   Most game creation choices are limited to 1 selection.
4)   You can only research 10 items.
5)   You cannot build the most advanced units.
6)   In network games your player attributes are reduced 10%.
7)   You can only play the first 5 scenarios.
8)   In non-scenario play you can only play for 3 hours.
9)   You can save, but you cannot load a game. Saved games can be loaded in the retail version.
